Dasharo OSF vPub Winter 2022

17 February 2022

8 PM UTC - last haker standing

Calendar Invites: iCal / Google


  • Dasharo OSF vPub Matrix room in Dasharo Workspace (Matrix) with embedded Jitsi conference using Element.
    Following configuration similar to virtual FOSDEM, we added integration of Jitsi to our Dasharo OSF vPub Matrix channel.
    If you don't have Matrix account, here is a quick guide on how to get started with Element & Matrix or feel free to use Jitsi directly (link below).
  • Jitsi Conference Link - please click to join Jitsi audio (optional video) conference via web browser or mobile app
  • Dasharo Workspace (Matrix) - join #dasharo-osf-vpub:matrix.org optional Matrix channel for text chat
  • Dasharo OSF vPub Newsletter - if you would like to be informed about future vPub events feel free to sign up to our newsletter


4th edition of vPub is approaching! We will be discussing lots of interesting topics with many great guests!

vPub schedule

Time [UTC] Description
19:30 - 19:50 Channel open for testing
19:50 - 20:00 Welcome to vPub
20:00 - 20:10 Dennis Marttinen will introduce us to Racklet project.
20:10 - 20:30 Q&A: Racklet
20:30 - 21:00 Open Discussion
21:00 - 21:10 Michiel Leenaars will give us short introduction to Technology Commons Trust as a vehicle for open source firmware and hardware crowdfunding.
21:10 - 21:30 Q&A: Open source firmware and hardware crowdfunding
21:30 - 22:00 Open Discussion
22:00 - 22:10 Luke Kenneth Casson Leighton will dive into comparative debugging for libre-soc by deliberately tracking microwatt source and unit tests.
22:10 - 22:30 Q&A: LibreSoC and Microwatt
22:30 - 23:00 Open Discussion
23:00 - 23:10 Daniel Maslowski will let us know about current status of oreboot project.
23:10 - 23:30 Q&A: oreboot
23:30 - 00:00 Open Discussion
00:00 - 01:00 Discussion with Richard Stallman about Respect Your Freedom Certification
01:00 - 01:10 Felix Singer and tonux will explain how open source firmware community can benefit from GSoC 2022.
01:10 - 01:30 Q&A: GSoC 2022 for open source firmware
01:30 - last haker standing Open Discussion:
- Crowdfunding of open source firmware distribution for modern platforms ,
- Public review of the open letter draft to open the Intel ACMs code ,
- Open source firmware distribution through fwupd/LVFS ,
- Dasharo project statuses: KGPE-D16 , OpenPOWER , Dell OptiPlex , PC Engines apu2 , modern laptops support NovaCustom,
- OpenSecurityTraining2 reward system plans and ideas,
- Recent advancements in Root of Trust technologies (MS Pluton announcements, TrenchBoot project status)
